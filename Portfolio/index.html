About Me

Hello! I'm Ryan Oquias, a passionate game developer with expertise in design and programming. I specialize in creating immersive gaming experiences that combine compelling storytelling with innovative gameplay mechanics.


With several years of experience in the gaming industry, I've worked on various projects ranging from indie mobile games to AAA titles. My goal is to create games that not only entertain but also leave a lasting impression on players.


When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring new game releases, participating in game jams, or sketching new character concepts.
